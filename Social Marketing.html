About Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is an internet marketing process to achieve marketing communication and branding by using different social media platforms. Social media marketing primarily covers a range of activities involving social sharing of content, videos, and images for marketing purposes both organically and by paid ad.

Benefits of social media marketing

  • Building Brand
  • Raising Brand awareness
  • Increasing engagement
  • Building-up relationship with audience in long term
  • Earning revenue with low investment
